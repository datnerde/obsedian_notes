## STAR Framework Reminder

- **S**ituation: brief context (1–2 sentences)
- **T**ask: what was your responsibility
- **A**ction: what *you specifically* did — use "I", not "we"
- **R**esult: quantified impact where possible

---

## High-frequency BQ Topics

These come up in nearly every DS/MLE interview:

- **Conflict / disagreement** → Story 9 (Rule vs ML)
- **Failure / mistake** → Story 8 (Tech Debt)
- **Influence without authority** → Story 1 (WS Pushback)
- **Ambiguity** → Story 3 (WS Model Ambiguity)
- **Data ethics / bad news** → Story 2 (Remodel Negative Result)
- **Mentorship** → Story 5 (Coaching colleague)

---

## Interview Mindset

> Treat the interview as pair-programming with a future colleague, not as an exam.

- Ask clarifying questions first (don't jump to answer)
- Think out loud — verbalize your reasoning
- For behavioral: tell the story, then connect it to what it shows about how you work
- End each answer with what you *learned* or would do differently — it shows growth mindset
